about

Hi and welcome to whatever this is.

My name is Baird. I'm a climate scientist working in energy transition research at Global Energy Monitor, where I manage oil and gas infrastructure databases. I'm interested in maps and design, and weather and climate, and I've been teaching myself how to make things online lately. I believe very strongly in open-source and creative commons stuff.

I studied geophysics in college and then did graduate work in atmospheric and oceanic science. After about a decade in academia, I shifted to work as an academic editor at Nature Climate Change, where I was before I began my current role.

I've spent a lot of my career using Python and matplotlib to wrangle and visualize data. In my free time, I'm attempting to improve my data visualization and mapping skills, and maybe give a go at some low-stakes data journalism projects. Check back later for more.
