about

Hi, and welcome to whatever this is. : )

My name is Baird. I'm a climate scientist working in energy transition research at Global Energy Monitor, where I manage oil and gas infrastructure databases. I'm interested in maps and web design, and I've been teaching myself how to make things online lately.

I work from home in Brooklyn, NY.

I studied geophysics in college and then did graduate work in atmospheric and oceanic science. After about a decade in academia, I shifted to work as an academic editor at Nature Climate Change, and I went on to energy research after that.

I've spent a lot of my career using Python and matplotlib to wrangle and visualize data. I'm trying to use 2024 to improve my online data visualization and mapping know-how, and maybe give a go at some low-stakes data journalism projects. We'll see how it goes.
